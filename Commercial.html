Commercial Aircraft

Freight and Cargo

Here at AAC, we know our customers demand as much from our line of products as we demand of ourselves. We have modified an Airbus 321 to best suit your needs. It’s the world’s first single-aisle freighter with Fly-By-Wire, enabling Cross-Crew Qualification and Mixed-Fleet Flying with all other Airbus aircraft types. This technology, combined with the low fuel burn and emissions, make the A321 our choice. We have modified the freight area and upgraded the engines to allow for 2 additional tonnes of payload and 200 km extra range over the standard.

  • Length: 44.51 m
  • Wing span: 35.8 m
  • Max payload: 30 tonnes (2 tonne increase over the standard A321)
  • Max range: 4000 km (200 km above standard)
